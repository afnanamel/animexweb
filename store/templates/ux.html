What is UI/UX?

UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) are two distinct but interconnected disciplines crucial to designing digital products. UI design focuses on the visual and interactive elements of a product, such as its layout, colors, and buttons, while UX design is the broader process of planning the entire user's interaction, ensuring the product is easy, logical, and enjoyable to use.

In essence, UI is the "how it looks," and UX is the "how it feels and functions." Together they create products that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and user-friendly.
